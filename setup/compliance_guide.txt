FSI ADVISORY COMPLIANCE GUIDE — Premium Desk
Version 2.1 | Effective: January 2026

1. SUITABILITY REQUIREMENTS
All investment recommendations must align with the client's documented risk profile. Suitability assessments must be renewed annually or within 30 days of a material life event (marriage, divorce, birth of child, retirement, inheritance).

Risk profile classifications and equity exposure limits:
- Conservative: Maximum 15% in variable income (equities, multimarket funds)
- Moderate: Maximum 40% in variable income
- Mod-Aggressive: Maximum 60% in variable income
- Aggressive: Maximum 80% in variable income

COMPLIANCE ALERT THRESHOLD: Any client with equity exposure exceeding their profile limit by more than 5 percentage points triggers an automatic compliance flag requiring immediate advisor review and documented justification within 3 business days.

2. SUITABILITY RENEWAL
Suitability documents expire 12 months after signature. Clients with expired suitability may not receive new product recommendations until renewal is completed. The system generates alerts 30 days before expiry. Failure to renew within 15 days of expiry triggers a regulatory reporting obligation.

3. DISCLOSURE OBLIGATIONS
Advisors must disclose all fees, conflicts of interest, and product risks before any transaction. Verbal disclosures during recorded calls are accepted as valid for standard products. Written disclosure is mandatory for structured products, derivatives, and any product with lock-up periods exceeding 360 days.

4. CONCENTRATION RISK
Single-asset concentration above 30% of portfolio value requires documented client acknowledgment signed within 5 business days. For VIP clients (AUM above R$5M), the concentration threshold is 40%. Concentration above 50% in any single asset class is prohibited regardless of client profile.

5. WITHDRAWAL RISK INDICATORS
ElevenAgent monitors the following behavioral signals for redemption risk:
- Client uses language indicating intent to withdraw ("pull out", "reduce", "take some out") during recorded calls
- Three or more redemptions in a 60-day period
- Sentiment score below 30 on AI call analysis
- Portfolio NAV decline of more than 8% in 30 days

When any two signals are present simultaneously, the advisor must schedule a follow-up call within 5 business days and document the outcome.

6. LIFE EVENT PROTOCOL
When a client discloses a material life event (birth of child, retirement, health event), the advisor must:
a) Update the client's financial objectives within 10 business days
b) Trigger a new suitability assessment
c) Review and adjust the portfolio if the current allocation no longer fits the updated profile

7. VOICE MESSAGE AND AI COMMUNICATION STANDARDS
All client communications generated by AI assistants must be reviewed and approved by the licensed advisor before delivery. Voice messages must:
- Accurately represent product terms and risk
- Not contain guarantees of return or misleading performance comparisons
- Be archived for a minimum of 5 years per regulatory requirement
- Be delivered only through approved channels (WhatsApp Business API, email, SMS)

8. PROHIBITED PRACTICES
- Recommending products outside the client's documented risk profile without an explicit signed waiver
- Executing trades without documented client confirmation (verbal on recorded line or written)
- Sharing client data across accounts without explicit written consent
- Sending AI-generated communications without advisor review and approval
- Contacting clients outside business hours (8am-8pm local time) without prior consent
